Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PrepEdge AI

Our AI engine will instantly build a custom interview session for you. If you selected a Job Title, it curates questions based on the role, experience level, and category. If you uploaded a Resume, it extracts your specific skills and past experiences to challenge you just like a real recruiter would.
Experience Level (e.g., Fresher vs Senior) changes the focus—freshers get more foundational and theoretical questions, while seniors get architecture and leadership scenarios. Difficulty (Easy to Hard) scales the depth and complexity expected in your answers for those topics.
Once generated, click "Start Mock Interview." You'll be prompted to answer each question aloud using your microphone. The system transcribes your speech in real-time, then our AI evaluates your answer against the ideal response.
If you get stuck, you can click to reveal a Hint. The hint will guide you toward the ideal answer framework, but using it may slightly reduce your maximum possible AI score for that specific question.
After answering, you'll see instant feedback, a score out of 100, and areas for improvement. You can review all past sessions, track your performance over time, and export full PDF reports from the Session History and Dashboard tabs.
Upload your PDF or DOCX resume and our AI will automatically detect your target role, analyze your resume against ATS (Applicant Tracking System) criteria, and generate a detailed score with sub-scores for structure, keywords, readability, completeness, and role match. Interview questions are then tailored directly to your resume content and skills.
Our AI uses advanced language models to evaluate your spoken answers against ideal responses. It checks for accuracy, completeness, clarity, and relevance. While no AI is perfect, the feedback is designed to mirror what a real interviewer would look for — focusing on key concepts, structure, and communication quality.
Absolutely. Your resume data, session history, and transcripts are stored securely in your account and are never shared with third parties. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your profile settings. Voice recordings are processed in real-time and are not stored after transcription.
PrepEdge AI supports virtually any job role — from Software Developer, Data Analyst, and DevOps Engineer to Marketing Manager, Product Designer, and HR Specialist. The more specific your job title (e.g., "React.js Frontend Developer" instead of just "Developer"), the more tailored and relevant your questions will be.
Focus on structuring your answers clearly — start with a brief overview, then dive into details with real examples. Speak at a moderate pace to ensure accurate transcription. Review the "Ideal Answer" after each question to understand what was expected, and use the AI feedback to identify your weak areas. Regular practice with different difficulty levels will significantly boost your scores over time.
